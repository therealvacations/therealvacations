Questions? We've Got Answers

How do deposits and payment plans work?

Secure your spot with a deposit, then make flexible payments on your own schedule. When you get to the booking page, it will show you exactly what's required based on when you book and the balance due date for your trip. No credit checks, no interest.

How does the spin wheel discount work?

Click "Spin for My Discount" anywhere on the site. The wheel spins immediately — no signup required. Whatever it lands on is your prize, from $50 off to $500 off. After spinning, enter your name and email to claim your code. It's emailed to you instantly. Enter it at checkout when booking. Codes are single-use, valid for 48 hours.

Can I travel solo or do I need a group?

Come solo! Many of our travelers come alone and leave with lifelong connections. That's the magic of group travel.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled within 24 hours of booking. After 24 hours, deposits are non-refundable but transferable to another traveler with a $50 fee. Travel insurance is highly recommended.

How do I get notified when new trips open?

Click "Join the Interest List" on any upcoming trip. You'll be first to know when booking opens — with exclusive early-bird pricing.
