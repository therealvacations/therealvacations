About The Real Vacations

Creating unforgettable group travel experiences since 2006

Our Story

The Real Vacations was born from a simple idea: travel shouldn't be complicated, scary, or lonely. In 2006, we started organizing trips for a small group of friends who wanted to explore the world together but didn't have the time or expertise to plan it all.

What began as a handful of friends turned into hundreds of travelers discovering new destinations, experiencing new cultures, and building lifelong friendships. Today, we're one of the most trusted group travel companies, known for curating authentic experiences that celebrate culture, community, and adventure.

Over 20 years, we've learned that the best trips aren't just about the destination—they're about the people, the stories, and the connections made along the way. That's what we create: real vacations for real people.
