Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What's included in the trip cost?
Trip costs include accommodations, main events/activities, most meals, ground transportation, and 24/7 travel support. Flights are typically separate unless noted. Check each trip's details for specifics.
Can I get a refund if I cancel?
Yes! Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund. Cancellations 15-29 days out forfeit 50%. Less than 15 days, no refund is possible. Travel insurance is available for added protection.
What if I need to change my travel dates?
Contact us as soon as possible. We'll try to move you to another trip date if available. If your original trip is full, you may have limited options, so reach out early.
Are flights included?
Most trips don't include flights—this lets you book from your preferred location and airline. Some all-inclusive trips do include flights; check the trip details. We can help you coordinate with other travelers.
How many people are on a typical trip?
Group sizes vary from 15-75 people depending on the trip. Smaller groups offer more intimacy; larger groups bring more energy. Each trip listing shows the expected group size.
Will I know anyone on my trip?
You might! But most travelers are meeting their group for the first time. We create a private group chat before departure so everyone can connect and introduce themselves.
What happens if the trip gets cancelled?
We rarely cancel trips. If we must due to unforeseen circumstances, we offer a full refund or credit toward a future trip. We'll work with you to reschedule.
How do I get travel tips and stay updated?
Once booked, you'll get email updates with packing lists, destination guides, itinerary changes, and group chat access. Check your trip dashboard anytime for the latest info.
