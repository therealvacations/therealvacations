How It Works

From discovery to departure — here's everything you need to know about booking your unforgettable group trip with us

Six Simple Steps to Your Dream Trip

Book, pay, travel, and create memories with The Real Vacations

1

Explore Our Trips

Browse our curated selection of group trips. Each one is handpicked for amazing experiences, great vibes, and unforgettable memories.

2

Choose Your Trip

Found the one? Review the itinerary, dates, price, and group details. Make sure it fits your vibe and your schedule.

3

Secure Your Spot

Book now and lock in your place. We'll collect a deposit to hold your spot. Spots fill up fast on our best trips.

4

Make Payments

Pay the remainder on a flexible schedule before the deadline. We offer payment plans to make it easier on your budget.

5

Get Ready & Connect

Access your trip dashboard. Download the itinerary, connect with your travel group, and get travel tips to prepare.

6

Travel & Enjoy

Show up, embrace the experience, and make memories with your group. This is what it's all about.
