Why This Trip is Unforgettable

For over 30 years, Essence Festival has been the ultimate celebration of Black culture, music, and empowerment. More than just concerts, it's a cultural movement that brings together 400,000+ attendees from around the world for three days of powerful performances, inspiring speakers, and joyful community.

From Grammy-winning headliners on the mainstage to thought-provoking panels at the Convention Center, Essence Fest offers a transformative experience. And there's no better place for it than New Orleans—a city where culture, history, and music flow through every street.

With our all-inclusive package, you'll experience the best of both worlds: the energy of the festival and the magic of NOLA. We handle the logistics so you can focus on making memories, connecting with your group, and celebrating Black excellence in all its forms.
