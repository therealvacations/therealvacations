Why This Trip is Unforgettable

Mary J Blige isn't just performing—she's closing out a chapter. Her "My Life, My Story" residency at Dolby Live has been a raw, emotional, and powerful celebration of her three-decade career. This finale is your chance to witness the Queen of Hip-Hop Soul in one of her most intimate performances yet.

Dolby Live at Park MGM is unlike any other venue in Vegas. The acoustics are pristine, the sightlines are perfect, and the immersive sound technology makes you feel like you're inside the music. With only 5,300 seats, every spot in the house is a good one.

And you're not just seeing a show—you're staying steps away. Park MGM is connected directly to the venue, so you can enjoy pre-show drinks, catch the performance, and be back in your room within minutes. The VIP experience at The Reserve takes it even further with a private lounge, elevated service, and luxury that matches the occasion.
